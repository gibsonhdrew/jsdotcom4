About Performance + Installation Film

Film

Handwritten

A personal reflection on the significance of handwriting in my own life and throughout history, sparked by the fact that Jaime has never had a consistent handwriting style.

Equality Tea

A film where Jaime brews tea while drawing parallels between the fraught histories of the tea trade and the suffrage movement.

Color Theory

A film that examines the perception of color throughout the history of Western art and culture as a subtext for how we understand race.

Specially Processed American Me

A filmed theatrical production of a surreal autobiographical performance using SPAM, the canned meat, as a portal into Jaime Sunwoo's Asian American upbringing and her family's experiences of the Korean War.
